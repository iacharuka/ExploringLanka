Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay a deposit?

No deposit is required to get a quote. We typically collect a 20% deposit upon confirmation of your itinerary.

What's the best time to visit?

Dec–March is best for the south coast. Jun–Sep for the east coast. Safari seasons vary by park — ask us!

Are your tours completely private?

Yes — every single tour is exclusively yours. No shared vans, no group strangers, ever.

Can I customize any tour?

Absolutely. Every tour on our website is a starting point. We'll modify any aspect to match your preferences.

Do you handle visa arrangements?

We provide full guidance on Sri Lanka ETA visa application. Most nationalities can apply online at eta.gov.lk.

What payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfer, PayPal, Wise, credit card (via Stripe). Cash on arrival also accepted for some tours.
