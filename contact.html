FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Simple answers for tourists using ExploringLanka.

How can I book a tour?

Click the Book Now button on a tour card, fill the booking form, and send your details through WhatsApp or email.

Do I need to pay online?

This demo website does not include online payments. Booking confirmation is handled through WhatsApp or email.

Can I customize my tour?

Yes. Use the Custom Tour page to request a private itinerary based on your destinations, dates, budget, and travel style.

Do you provide airport transfers?

Yes. You can request airport pickups, hotel transfers, city-to-city transport, and private vehicles.

Are the tours private?

Many tours can be arranged as private experiences depending on availability, vehicle type, and traveler requirements.

Can I cancel my booking?

Cancellation conditions depend on the selected experience and will be confirmed during WhatsApp or email communication.
