Our Story

From a Land Rover
to 2,400+ Journeys

In 2012, Danushka and Ruwan — childhood friends from Kandy — started taking solo travelers on private jeep safaris through Yala. They had no website, no office, and exactly one vehicle. What they did have was an unmatched knowledge of Sri Lanka's wild places and a gift for making travelers feel like family.

Word spread. TripAdvisor ratings soared. Today, ExploringLanka operates a fleet of 12 vehicles, employs 18 full-time guides, and has welcomed travelers from 65+ countries. We've never had a shared tour. Every single journey is private.

"We don't sell tours. We share our home."

— Danushka, Founder
