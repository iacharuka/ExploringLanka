About ExploringLanka

Connecting travelers with unforgettable Sri Lankan experiences.

ExploringLanka is a modern Sri Lankan tourism marketplace concept built to help travelers discover private tours, airport transfers, cultural journeys, wildlife safaris, beach holidays, and custom island routes.

Our Story

Built for travelers who want Sri Lanka to feel easy, safe, and personal.

Sri Lanka is a small island with a huge variety of travel experiences: ancient kingdoms, sacred temples, misty mountains, wildlife parks, golden beaches, tea plantations, waterfalls, local food, and friendly people. But for many tourists, planning the right route can feel confusing.

ExploringLanka was designed as a tourism marketplace concept that brings tours, transfers, and custom trip planning into one clean frontend experience. Instead of a complex backend, this project uses WhatsApp and email booking so travelers can send their details directly and get personal support.

Project Purpose: This website demonstrates a real-world frontend booking flow using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WhatsApp links, email links, responsive design, and marketplace-style user interface patterns.

Why Sri Lanka?

One island. Many worlds.

Sri Lanka is one of the most diverse destinations in Asia. Within a short distance, travelers can move from ancient cities to mountain tea estates, from wildlife parks to tropical beaches, from sacred temples to modern city life.

Ancient Heritage Sigiriya, Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa, Dambulla, and Kandy.
Wildlife & Nature Yala, Udawalawe, elephants, leopards, birds, and national parks.
Beaches & Ocean Mirissa, Galle, Bentota, Trincomalee, Pasikuda, and whale watching.
Hill Country Ella, Nuwara Eliya, tea plantations, waterfalls, and scenic railways.
