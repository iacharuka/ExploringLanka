Featured Tour

Sigiriya Rock Fortress Guided Morning Tour

★★★★★ 4.9 • 2,500 reviews • Sigiriya • From $45 per person

Overview

Climb the iconic Lion Rock with a local guide and enjoy sunrise views over the jungle. This guided experience is ideal for first-time visitors who want history, photography, and a smooth morning plan.

Highlights

  • Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Learn from a local guide
  • See ancient frescoes and mirror wall
  • Enjoy panoramic jungle views
  • Optional hotel pickup

Included

Professional guide, hotel pickup if selected, bottled water, and parking support.

Not Included

Entrance tickets, meals, personal expenses, and tips.

Itinerary

Pickup → Arrival at Sigiriya → Guided climb → Viewpoints and photography → Return/drop-off.

Important Information

Wear comfortable shoes, bring water, and start early to avoid heat. Not recommended for travelers with serious mobility issues.
